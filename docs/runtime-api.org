** Webhook 与健康检查

当 =enable_scheduled_decision=true= 且 =[webhook].enabled=true= 时，以下路由挂载到顶层：

- =/healthz= — 健康检查
- =/api/live/freqtrade/webhook= — Freqtrade 回调接收

** 通知

支持两个消息渠道：

- Telegram Bot — 支持决策推送、命令交互（如 /observe）
- 飞书 Bot — 支持决策推送

系统启动后自动拉起已配置的 Bot。启动通知由 =startup_notify_enabled= 控制。

通知类型：
- 决策报告（图片卡片）— 每轮决策后推送
- 开仓通知（图片卡片）— 开仓时推送，包含入场价、止损、止盈
- 平仓通知（图片卡片）— 平仓时推送，重复通知自动合并
- 部分平仓通知（图片卡片）— 减仓时推送
- 风控更新通知（图片卡片）— 止损/止盈调整时推送
- 启动通知（图片卡片）— 系统就绪后推送，含币种、周期、余额
- 停止通知 — 系统关闭时推送，含运行时长
- 错误通知 — 严重错误时推送至配置的通知渠道

** Webhook & Health Checks

When =enable_scheduled_decision=true= and =[webhook].enabled=true=, these routes are mounted at the top level:

- =/healthz= — health check
- =/api/live/freqtrade/webhook= — Freqtrade callback receiver

** Notifications

Two messaging channels are supported:

- Telegram Bot — decision push and command interaction (e.g., /observe)
- Feishu Bot — decision push

Configured bots start automatically on launch. Startup notifications are controlled by =startup_notify_enabled=.

Notification types:
- Decision report (image card) — sent after each decision round
- Position open (image card) — sent on entry, showing entry price, stop loss, take profit
- Position close (image card) — sent on full close, duplicate notifications merged
- Partial close (image card) — sent on partial exit
- Risk update (image card) — sent on stop loss / take profit adjustment
- Startup (image card) — sent after system initialization, includes symbols, intervals, balance
- Shutdown — sent on graceful stop with uptime
- Error — critical errors pushed to configured channels
